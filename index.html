Who is Nel-Kevin ?

  • Originally from Haiti, Nel-Kevin Junior Dubois - KVN, have management and IT background.

Montreal Canada, KVN got spotted by producer, store's manager, brand's owner and many people in the fashion industry. This is where the real International Fashion carrer start for him.
